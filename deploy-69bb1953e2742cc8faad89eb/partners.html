The partner package

Everything a family needs - with your name on it.

01
Co-branded memorial page
Every family you serve gets a permanent, beautiful memorial. Your name sits alongside After Matters - visible every time family and friends visit, for years to come.
02
QR code on the programme
A QR code printed on the order of service links every attendee directly to the memorial. Every person at the funeral discovers After Matters through you.
03
Livestream hosting
For family overseas, elderly relatives who can't travel, and anyone who can't attend. A beautiful, private livestream. No technical setup required from your team.
04
Invitation management
Invitations sent through After Matters, RSVPs tracked, the inner circle coordinated. The family has one place for everything instead of juggling WhatsApp groups.
05
The Waypoint - estate guide
A step-by-step guide through every loose end after a death - IRD, bank, KiwiSaver, insurance, estate administration. NZ-specific, plain language, one task at a time.
06
Document vault access
If the person had an After Matters vault, their keyholder accesses everything through your co-branded platform. Our care team responds within 24 hours - always a real person.

Why After Matters exists

Built from real loss.

My dad died in fourteen days. Stomach cancer - diagnosis to funeral in two weeks. What followed was months of admin chaos while my mum grieved. The not-knowing, the paperwork, the institutions that had never heard the word empathy.

I built After Matters because that didn't have to be so hard. And I built the partner programme because funeral directors are the people families trust most in those first impossible days. You are the ones who could put something like this in their hands.

This isn't a technology company trying to disrupt the funeral industry. It's a daughter from Featherston who lost her dad and built something in his honour - so other families have it a little easier.

With care,Lola Bee - Founder, After Matters · Featherston, New Zealand
