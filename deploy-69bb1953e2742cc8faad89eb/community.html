How we created a family archive - photos, letters, voice recordings

I finally had the conversation with my parents - here's how
I'd been putting it off for two years. In the end I just said "I want to make sure I know what you want, because I love you." That's it. That's the opening. Mum cried a little. Dad made a joke. But we talked for three hours and now I know everything.

KiwiSaver after death - what I wish I'd known
It took 4 months and 11 phone calls to access dad's KiwiSaver. His beneficiary nomination was 20 years old. The provider was kind but the process was exhausting. Please check your nominations - and make sure your parents have checked theirs.
